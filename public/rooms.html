Rooms and Amenities

Best Eastern Hotel Chain has three locations (Verdun, Longueuil, and Laval). We generally have 4 types of rooms (Single Queen, Double Queen, King, and Suite).

Our Verdun location out of the three locations is the only location that provides some rooms that allow smoking and is the only location that has single queens.

All of our hotels have hot tub, swimming pool and a gym. All are open 24/7, and there are vending machines on-site.
